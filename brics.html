Website Development & Macroeconomics

Impact of Macroeconomic Variables on Brics

  • Category: Machine Learning
  • University: Georgetown University
  • Project date: 15 September, 2023
  • Project URL: https://sheebamoghal.georgetown.domains/_site/

Led end-to-end development for a comprehensive project, crafting the website's frontend with Quarto, CSS, SCSS, and HTML. On the backend, employed Python and R to unravel unexplored facets of BRICS's macroeconomic variables. Executed a thorough data pipeline encompassing cleaning, exploration, visualization, feature extraction, and engineering. Utilized a spectrum of supervised (naive bayes, linear regression, logistic regression, decision trees, and random forest) and unsupervised learning algorithms (Dimensionality Reduction - PCA, t-SNE, and Clustering - K-Means, DBSCAN, Hierarchical). Conducted sentiment analysis on textual data, culminating in effective classification.
