During my Data Science certification with Imarticus Learning, I collaborated with Senior Data Scientist Mr. Prashant Sharma from KPMG on an e-commerce project. Using K-Means and DBSCAN clustering, we segmented customers based on Recency, Frequency, and Monetary metrics, yielding silhouette scores of 0.58 and 0.45, respectively. Leveraging KNN and DBSCAN for customer analysis, we integrated findings with marketing strategies. This month-long project not only enhanced technical proficiency but also provided practical insights, emphasizing the importance of collaborative, real-world applications in data science.
