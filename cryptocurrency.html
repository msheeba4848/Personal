Cryptocurrency and Volatility Prediction

  • Category: Domain Analysis - Finance
  • Project date: 01 March, 2020
  • Project URL: GitHub

This study focused on forecasting the volatility of Monero, Ethereum, and Litecoin through the application of GARCH, AGARCH, and TARCH models. The goal was to identify the most suitable model for predicting cryptocurrency volatility, considering the unique characteristics of each digital asset. Evaluation metrics, particularly the Root Mean Squared Error (RMSE), consistently demonstrated accurate predictions, with RMSE values ranging between 0.15 to 0.10. The research emphasized the necessity of tailoring modeling techniques to the distinctive features of individual cryptocurrencies. The findings not only contribute to precise volatility prediction but also highlight the nuanced dynamics influencing Monero, Ethereum, and Litecoin. This research provides valuable insights for stakeholders navigating the complexities of digital asset markets, offering a robust framework for risk management and investment strategies.
